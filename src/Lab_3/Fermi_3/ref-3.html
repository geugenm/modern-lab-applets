1. Fermi-Dirac Integral

    Sommerfeld's definition

    Fn(h) = xn / [1 + e (x-h)]dx

    Dingle's definition [4]:

    Fn(h) = [1/G(n+1)] xn / [ 1 + e(x-h)]dx

    A.C.Bednarczyk and J.Bednarczyk, Phys. Lett. 64A, 409 (1978).
